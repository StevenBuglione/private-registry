# Third-party license intake

The first-party Registry UI bundles dependencies from `web/pnpm-lock.yaml`, local font files, and the Phosphor icon library. Generate an organization-approved dependency attribution report and software bill of materials during release. Preserve `NOTICE.md` for historical Registry evaluation provenance where required by legal policy.

Provider logo assets under `public/assets/providers/` identify their corresponding third-party products. AWS, Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud, Kubernetes, Helm, Datadog, Grafana, and associated marks remain the property of their respective owners. Their inclusion does not imply endorsement; confirm applicable trademark and redistribution policies before an external release.
